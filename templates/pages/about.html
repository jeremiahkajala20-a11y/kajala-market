About Kajala Market

Tanzania's Most Trusted Electronics Marketplace

Our Story

Kajala Market was founded in 2024 by Ramadhan Kajala with a simple mission: to provide Tanzanians with access to quality electronics at affordable prices. Starting from Dodoma, we have grown to become one of the most trusted online marketplaces in the country.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality technology without breaking the bank. That's why we work directly with verified sellers to ensure competitive prices and authentic products.

Our Mission

To connect buyers with trusted sellers, providing a seamless shopping experience with secure payments and reliable delivery across Tanzania.

Our Vision

To become Tanzania's leading e-commerce platform, empowering local businesses and providing customers with the best online shopping experience.
